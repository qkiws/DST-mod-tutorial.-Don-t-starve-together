# 🧸 Plush Bear Mod

Sometimes, surviving the constant darkness and dangers of the Constant is too stressful. You just need a soft friend to hold! This mod introduces a fully custom, craftable **Plush Bear** to keep you company.

### ✨ Features:
* **Custom Artwork & Animations:** Features a custom inventory icon, ground model, and in-hand animations.
* **Sanity Regeneration:** Just holding the bear provides a strong, comforting aura that restores your Sanity.
* **Health Recovery:** The bear literally heals your wounds, restoring your Health periodically while equipped.
* **Dynamic Light Source:** Scared of the dark? The Plush Bear automatically starts glowing with a warm light when night falls! It works when you hold it or when you drop it on the ground (but turns off to save energy when hidden in your inventory).
* **Balanced Durability:** The bear has a durability meter. It degrades slowly during the day, but burns durability faster at night when the light is active. 
* **Repairable:** Don't let your friend disappear! You can fully repair the Plush Bear using a standard **Sewing Kit**.

### 🛠️ Crafting:
Can be crafted using Beefalo Wool in the Survival tab.
